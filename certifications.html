Certifications & Compliance

Behind every badge is a real document.

The SGS testing, REACH compliance, and complete MSDS badges shown on the home page — this page explains exactly which documents each one corresponds to and which components they cover. Every order can request them as needed.

Certification & Compliance Framework
Six core certifications, covering the entire bundle process.
🧪
SGS Third-Party Testing
SGS third-party test reports available for key specs on polyester resin, curing agents, and more — covering national-standard-required acid value, hydroxyl value, softening point, melt viscosity and more.
Applies to: Resin · Curing Agent
🧾
REACH Compliance
For raw materials relevant to EU exports, we can provide REACH registration information and compliance declarations confirming exclusion of REACH-restricted substances or completed registration.
Applies to: All 5 Components
📄
MSDS Safety Data Sheet
Bilingual (Chinese/English) MSDS shipped with every order, covering physical/chemical properties, transport and storage requirements, and emergency handling procedures.
Applies to: All 5 Components
🌍
Certificate of Origin CO / FORM A
Available on request: China Certificate of Origin (CO) or Generalized System of Preferences Certificate (FORM A), to help customers access preferential tariff treatment in the importing country (subject to that country's actual policy).
Applies to: All 5 Components
✅
HAA Exportable to EU
The HAA curing agent system contains none of the TGIC-related SVHC-restricted substances, and complies with EU REACH regulations for exported powder coating curing agents.
Applies to: Curing Agent (HAA System)
🔬
Formula Compatibility Verification
Full bundle inquiry customers receive a formula compatibility verification report for the combined five components — see the joint verification items under Formula Risk Verification Services.
Applies to: Full Bundle Inquiries
