The Five Bundle Components
Not five products — one formula sheet.
Powder coating chemistry requires these five raw material categories on the same purchase order at the same time — miss one, and the formula doesn't work. We supply all five so you only have to deal with one contact.
Component 01
Polyester Resin System
45%
The largest share of the formula by weight, determining film flexibility and outdoor weatherability.
Available
Component 02
TGIC / HAA Curing Agent
5%
The core material that cross-links with the resin. HAA is EU-compliant; TGIC performs better but is restricted in the EU.
Available
Component 03
Leveling + Degassing Agents
1.5%
The smallest quantity but critical for surface finish — customers are most sensitive to this component's quality.
Available
Component 04
Titanium Dioxide (White Pigment)
3%
The core pigment for white and light-colored formulas. Rutile, chloride process, weatherable grade.
Available
Component 05
Barium Sulfate (Matting Filler)
35%
The largest-volume extender, controlling film gloss while managing overall formula cost.
