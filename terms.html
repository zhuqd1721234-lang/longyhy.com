1. Quality Guarantee Clause

Our formal order contracts include the following in writing — not just as verbal claims on this website:

  • After goods arrive at port, customers have the right to commission a local or third-party testing agency (such as SGS) to re-test against the contracted specifications;
  • If re-test results don't conform to the contracted specs (e.g. polyester resin acid value/hydroxyl value outside the agreed range, curing agent free chlorine exceeding limits), and both parties confirm the finding, we provide a full refund, replacement, or price adjustment;
  • Appearance or performance changes caused by transport or storage are excluded from this guarantee — the exact boundaries are defined in the contract;
  • Claims must be submitted within 30 calendar days of port arrival, along with a test report; claims made after this window are considered accepted without issue.
Note: Exact compensation ratios, choice of testing agency, and dispute resolution process are governed by the formal contract signed for each order. This page is a general explanation and doesn't constitute a contractual commitment for any specific order.

2. Escrow Payment Protection

For first-time customers or large orders, we support the following ways to reduce prepayment risk:

  • Escrow payment through a third-party trade platform (e.g. Alibaba's Trade Assurance orders), with funds released after acceptance;
  • Letter of credit (L/C) settlement, in line with standard international trade practice;
  • Split payment: deposit + balance before shipment + retention after port acceptance — exact ratios negotiated based on order value.

Escrow payment isn't mandatory for every order — small sample orders and long-term, stable partners can negotiate more flexible payment terms, as agreed in the contract between both parties.

3. Consolidated Shipping

For the five bundle components in a full bundle inquiry, if a single order's total volume isn't enough to fill a container on its own, we procure and warehouse according to your specified formula ratio and ship them together in one container. The resulting freight and customs costs are calculated and allocated by us and reflected in the final quote, with no extra consolidation fee.

4. Privacy Policy

What information we collect: When you submit our inquiry forms, we receive the name, company name, email, WhatsApp number (if provided), and formula/requirement details you enter.

How this information is used: Solely to respond to your inquiry, provide a quote, and follow-up business communication — never for any purpose beyond this specific inquiry.

How this information is transmitted: Submitting an inquiry form opens your own device's email client and sends the form content as an email to our business inbox; form content is not stored or relayed through any third-party server.

How this information is protected: We will not sell, rent, or provide your contact details or inquiry content to any third party unrelated to this business.

Your rights: You may contact us at any time via the details below to request an explanation, correction, or deletion of any inquiry information we've received.

Scope: This policy applies to information collected through the inquiry forms on this site (puretitan-consulting.com).
