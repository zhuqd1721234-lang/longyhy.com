Our Focus

We only do two things — but we do them all the way.

Curing agents (TGIC/HAA) and polyester resin are the two most technically demanding, quality-sensitive parts of a powder coating formula bundle — every batch needs an on-site supplier audit, batch-by-batch testing of key specs, and formula compatibility verification. We only do these two lines, which is exactly why we can deliver on the "slow work" others don't have time for.

What We Can Do For You
Focus isn't a slogan — it shows up in these four things.
These four things are hard for small trading intermediaries to pull off, and large factories don't have the bandwidth for them either — and they're exactly what we want you to understand before we work together.
On-Site Supplier Audits
Not just a qualification document — we go verify on-site
Reactor specs, monthly capacity, recent production records — this data can only be verified on the factory floor; paper qualifications can be outdated or mismatched with actual capacity. This is the most commonly pre-purchased item among our formula risk verification services.
Batch-by-Batch Testing
We check the core specs mandated by national standard before problems arise, not after
Resin is tested per batch on 8 national-standard specs including softening point, acid value, and hydroxyl value; curing agent is checked for melting point crystal ratio and free chlorine content — intercepting the risks that ruin a whole batch of film at the source, instead of tracing back after a customer complaint.
Formula Compatibility Verification
Before switching suppliers or curing agent systems, we test it first so you don't have to guess
Switching TGIC to HAA, adjusting resin acid value — changing the formula ratio without verifying compatibility first is a common cause of a whole batch being scrapped. We manage resin and curing agent together, so we can respond quickly to your urgent restocking needs while still running compatibility tests before any switch.
Transparent Pricing, Not Guesswork
Every dollar can be broken down for you to see
Labor hours, testing fees, travel costs — every one of our 12 verification services is priced against real cost accounting, and we can walk you through every line item. We won't quote you off the cuff just because you're not familiar with the market.
