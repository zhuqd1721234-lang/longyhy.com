Polyester Resin Related
The largest share of the formula — and the costliest to get wrong.
Resin makes up 40-48% of the formula, is the highest-volume, highest-negotiating-weight component, and is subject to mandatory batch-by-batch national standard testing — fail any one spec, and the whole batch is rejected. These four services revolve around "is the capacity real" and "are batches consistent."
